Corrupted Nexus

Several maps with Coalesced Corruption will surround a map indicated as a Corrupted Nexus. The Corrupted Nexus will always contain a Beyond boss. In order to access a Corrupted Nexus, a waystone with at least 4 modifiers must be used.

Completing the Corrupted Nexus map will cause all nearby maps with Coalesced Corruption to become Cleansed maps instead, causing different monsters to appear.

After completing a Corrupted Nexus of an appropriate waystone tier, Doryani will provide you a Crystalline Shard which can be used to gain points for the Atlas Passive Tree as part of the endgame progression system.

Uniques

  • The Gnashing Sash
  • Death Articulated
  • Bursting Decay

After defeating Corrupted Nexus bosses, any remaining areas within the Corrupted Zone will be cleansed. Cleansed maps feature Irradiated with additional new monsters, a new boss, and a set of unique affixes and rewards.
