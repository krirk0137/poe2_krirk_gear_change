Introduction

This Spirit Walker Twisters Build Guide for the Huntress class is capable of massive single target and clear using the same combo. This guide takes you from level 1 all the way into the endgame. You get both of your main skills at level 1, so you can begin your endgame build right away! Whirling Slash spins up a storm around you. After three attacks, you unleash a volley of Twisters. Barrage gives you more tornadoes, and Ice-Tipped Arrows turns twisters Cold, unleashing devastation on packs of monsters and bosses alike.


Defensively, the Spirit Walker Twisters build uses high amounts of Evasion and Deflection get hit less and be able to tank more damage. Your Summoned Bear also takes significant damage for you.


This build does not require any uniques to function, making it a great league starter. However, The Black Insignia helmet provides access to Tailwind for speed and Evasion, while usually being very cheap. In the endgame, Hyrri's Ire provides massive damage, utility and defence.

Equipment

Learn how to optimise your Spirit Walker Twisters Build Guide with the optimal Skills, Rotation, Ascendancy, Passive tree, and Gear in Path of Exile 2.

  • Weapon Set 1 is always prioritizing the most amount of attack speed possible for Whirling Slash. Weapon Set 2 is looking for the most flat damage (prefixes) possible, not necessarily DPS. DPS calculations include Attack Speed and that can inflate the effectiveness of the weapon. Additional levels for Projectile Skills is also very powerful.
  • Prioritize Life and Resistance on gear as much as possible.
  • Getting as much Armour or Armour/Evasion as possible on gear will make the campaign much smoother. Evasion by itself feels rough early, and deflection is hard to get early on.
  • Use an Omen Sceptre as soon as possible in Weapon Set II. This provides Malice for additional Critical Hit Chance. This item can drop in Level 16 or higher zones.
  • Weapon Set 1 is always prioritizing the most amount of attack speed possible for Whirling Slash. Weapon Set 2 is looking for the most flat damage (prefixes) possible, not necessarily DPS. DPS calculations include Attack Speed and that can inflate the effectiveness of the weapon. Additional levels for Projectile Skills and Critical Suffixes are also very powerful.
  • Getting as much Evasion and Deflection on Body Armour, Gloves, Boots and Helmet is critical to your defenses.
  • Get a Valako's Roar charm as soon as you can afford one (or drop one). They are very common and provide a boost to frenzy charge generation.
  • Weapon Set 1 is always prioritizing the most amount of attack speed possible for Whirling Slash. Weapon Set 2 is looking for the most flat damage (prefixes) possible, not necessarily DPS. DPS calculations include Attack Speed and that can inflate the effectiveness of the weapon. Getting a powerful weapon is vital. Triple flat damage prefixes, preferably Physical and Cold damage, are ideal. For Suffixes, high Critical chance is a must.
  • Getting as much Evasion and Deflection on Body Armour, Gloves, Boots and Helmet is critical to your defenses.

Skills

However, if you leave the whirlwind before casting Twister, the whirlwind collapses and you lose the extra damage. Be sure to always cast Twister while inside the whirlwind. ALWAYS leave Whirling Slash at level 1, as it saves a ton of mana.

Rotation

Learn more about the Skill Rotation for the Spirit Walker Twisters Build Guide here.

Clearing

For general clearing, Ice-Tipped Arrows converts your Twister damage to cold and allow freezing and the creation of chilled ground from the support gem Frost Nexus. Whirling Slash often isnt even necessary, as the ice fragments created by Ice-Tipped Arrows clears most mobs for you. On more dense packs, three slashes and a cast of Barrage handles the tough rares and clear offscreen.

Bossing - Act 1

In Act 1, Parry and Fangs of Frost adds chilled ground for huge bonus damage for Twister. Use Whirling Slash three times, then Twister to quickly burst down bosses.


Bossing - Act II and Beyond

Bossing uses a similar skill rotation to mapping, but you'll always want to use Whirling Slash three times first for maximum damage. Drop your War Banner and use Freezing Mark for additional debuffs and damage. Barrage is huge bonus damage, especially if you have created Frenzy Charges from Combat Frenzy.

    • You can use your Gold at the Hooded one or Doryani to respec and change your Attributes if a new skill or gear upgrade leaves you unable to use your skills/items.
  • Your Weapon Swap trees are VERY important. Weapon Set I is for fast attacking, so you can whip up Whirling Slash faster and move around quickly. You should also put Barrage here so you get the skill speed benefits of Dance with Death. Lastly, use War Banner on Weapon Set I to save on Spirit. Weapon Set II is for all other skills. Ice-Tipped Arrows needs to be on 2 because otherwise it won't convert your Twister damage. Weapon Set I takes as much attack speed as possible on the tree, while Weapon Set II takes as much damage as possible, ignoring speed. Use a powerful attack speed Spear in Weapon Set I and a high flat damage Spear in Weapon Set II.

FAQ

Take a look at this section for a deeper dive on Frequently Asked Questions, common mistakes and the underlying mechanics of how the Spirit Walker Twisters Build Guide works.

  • Make sure your Elemental Resists are at 75% and around 60%+ Chaos resistance. Furthermore, make sure you have the appropriate bases equipped to gain as much Evasion and Energy Shield in the right spots as possible.

    Playstyle is also important. Do not try to Whirling Slash if you are completely surrounded. Move to a safer location to deal damage.

  • Ice-Tipped Arrows must be on Weapon Set II with Twister. Only Barrage and Whirling Slash should be set to Weapon Set I.

  • Weapon Set I is designed to maximize attack speed for Whirling Slash and Weapon Set II is designed for maximizing damage from Twister. This allows you to use Dance with Death for skill speed, and at the same time have an Omen Sceptre in your Twister Weapon Set II for additional Critical chance.

Summary

  • Twister is a screen clearing machine with the power to take down massive bosses thanks to Spirit Walker's projectile synergy.
  • Freeze and chill your enemies to keep them from hitting you. When the enemies get close, you are protected by Evasion, Deflect, and Energy Shield.
  • Shatter packs with Ice Fragments from Ice-Tipped Arrows
  • Smooth fast clear allows this to be one of the fastest league starters.
